FAQ

Got questions?

Is Zyrobe free to use? ›
Yes! Zyrobe will be free to use upon release. We will also offer a premium tier with advanced features like AI-powered style recommendations and unlimited outfit saves.
How does the closet digitization work? ›
Simply take a photo of your clothing item. Our AI automatically removes the background, categorizes the item by type, color, and season, and adds it to your digital closet.
Can I plan outfits for the week ahead? ›
Absolutely! Use the outfit planner to create and schedule looks for each day. You can also get weather-based suggestions and set reminders.
Is my data secure? ›
Your privacy is our priority. All wardrobe data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time.
Which platforms is the app available on? ›
Zyrobe is currently in development and will soon be available on both Android and iOS. Stay tuned!
